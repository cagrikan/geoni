# GEONI (geoni.ai)

> GEONI, markaların yapay zeka arama motorlarında (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude vb.) görünürlüğünü, atıf oranlarını ve itibarını optimize eden bir Üretken Motor Optimizasyonu (GEO - Generative Engine Optimization) platformu ve ajansıdır.

## Core Capabilities & Services

- **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO):** Optimizing brand presence within AI search engines and Large Language Models (LLMs).
- **AI Audit & Simulation:** Comprehensive analysis of how AI search engines perceive, score, and recommend a brand across specific industry queries.
- **Entity & Authority Optimization:** Establishing and reinforcing strong brand entity relations on structured data networks (Wikidata, Schema) to boost LLM trust score.
- **Citation & Referral Acquisition:** Strategies to secure explicit citations and referral links inside AI-generated responses (e.g., via HARO, digital PR, high-authority mentions).
- **LLM.txt & Machine-Readable Content Setup:** Implementing architecture designed specifically for AI crawlers to efficiently parse, understand, and cite brand information.

## Service Packages

- **AI Audit (One-time):** Deep-dive situational analysis, AI share-of-voice reporting, and a foundational GEO roadmap.
- **Growth (Monthly Subscription):** Ongoing entity optimization, prompt simulation maps, citation tracking, and continuous LLM-ready content engineering.
- **Enterprise (Custom):** Dedicated GEO operations, predictive trend modeling, multi-brand dashboard management, and prioritized technical support.

## Key Technical Specifications

- **Focus Engine:** LLMs, Chatbots, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems.
- **Methodology:** Source Authority, Structural Data Enrichment, Real-time Prompt Testing, Entity Freshness Alignment.
